# BodyCheck — Product Brief

## Product
**BodyCheck** is a clean, no-friction web app that instantly calculates BMI (Body Mass Index) and explains what the number means in plain, friendly language. No accounts. No tracking. Just type in your height and weight, get your number, understand it.

## Problem
Most BMI calculators online are cluttered with ads, gated behind sign-ups, or spit out a number with no context. People want a fast, honest, judgment-free answer to "what's my BMI and what does it mean?"

## Audience
Health-conscious adults:
- Fitness beginners starting a journey
- Weight-trackers checking in
- Casual users curious about where they land
- People who want a fast answer without creating yet another account

**Explicitly: no login required.** The whole experience works in seconds, anonymously.

## P0 Feature — Instant BMI Calculation
The single non-negotiable feature on launch:
- Input fields for **height** and **weight**
- A **metric / imperial toggle** (cm + kg vs ft/in + lbs)
- Output:
  - BMI number (one decimal)
  - Category: Underweight / Healthy / Overweight / Obese
  - A short, friendly interpretation (1–2 sentences) of what the category means
- Instant calculation as the user types (no submit button friction)
- Clear visual feedback for the category (color-coded, but tasteful)

## Brand & Tone
- **Friendly, light, encouraging.** Never clinical or scary.
- **Informs without judging.** A "healthy" result isn't a gold star; an "overweight" result isn't a scolding. Every category gets a calm, supportive blurb.
- **Honest about limits.** BMI is a rough indicator — the app should gently note this without burying the result.

## Anti-References
- NOT a medical diagnostic tool — no fear-mongering, no "consult a doctor immediately" alarms
- NOT a weight-loss app — no calorie counting, meal plans, or workout suggestions
- NOT account-gated — no sign-up walls, no email capture, no newsletter prompts
- NOT ad-heavy — clean, focused, respectful of attention

## Strategic Principles
1. **Speed over depth.** The result should appear within 2 seconds of opening the page.
2. **One job, done well.** Resist feature creep — BMI calculation is the entire product.
3. **Anonymous by default.** No login, no tracking, no data persistence required.
4. **Tone is the moat.** The friendly, non-judgmental voice is what makes this different from a generic calculator.

## Success Looks Like
A user lands on the page, types two numbers, sees their BMI and a kind one-liner explaining it, and closes the tab feeling informed — not anxious, not sold to, not signed up for anything.
